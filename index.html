Goals

Safe Kids Worldwide looks to be a single repository for everyday awareness, learning and training for childhood preventive care. We need to continue that score value as we continue into the Information Technology era. In the past years the formulation and upkeep of such an organization might have been a large undertaking, and those who have immense knowledge to pass on might not have been able to transfer or store that knowledge for others to take part in, improve on, or continue to distribute. With advances in technology and the onset of the information age this concept can be changed for good. Safe Kids Worldwide looks to take advantage of these advancements in providing extra childhood injury awareness and create a distributable platform that families across the world can co-exist in doing the same task; preventing children fatalities and injuries. Areas of technology involvement are related to personalization profiling, IPAs ( intelligent personal assistants) , usage of the world wide web, and telepresence to help distribute the large data components at a speed and reliability rate that no other organization has been ever able to achieve.
