About Safe Kids

Safe Kids Worldwide is the worldâ€™s #1 and only truly dedicated platform for child preventative safety including a wide network establishment of organizations, partners, and countries involving corporate professionals, law enforcement, childhood educators, psychologists and more.

Safe Kids Worldwide works as an extensive network of partners and contributors as well as with other organizations to across 30 countries to help reduce fatal injuries, burns, drownings, and motor vehicle incidents. Established in 1988 along with founding partner Johnson & Johnson, Safe Kids has helped lower the rate of child injury by more than 50% through extensive learning and awareness programs. Each and everyday, throughout the world, almost roughly 1 million children are involved in a fatal injury each year. Our preventative discussions, materials, and research have and will continue to decrease this devastating events be making sure we are aware of the everyday threats for our children.

Safe Kids Worldwide is a global organization with a sole mission to assist in preventative protection for kids from unintentional incidents. Safe Kids Worldwide includes over 400 coalitions within the United States and partners within 30 countries around the world to help bring professional and educational awareness to those in needs on child preventative care. By providing statewide and country specific coalitions we are able to touch many families while providing a single preventative safety goal.
